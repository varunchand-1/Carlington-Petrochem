Introduction

Carlington Petrochem FZCO is a UAE-based entity specialising in pipeline trading, petrochemical solutions, and industrial materials for port development and global energy transportation. We are committed to providing durable, high-quality products that support infrastructure and industrial growth worldwide.

Core Activities

  • Delivering pipelines for oil and gas transportation (crude oil, refined products, LPG, and natural gas) with a focus on safety and efficiency.
  • Providing specialised solutions for industrial applications, including manufacturing and construction.

Petrochemical Trading

  • Crude Petroleum, Fuel Oil, and Liquefied Petroleum Gases (LPG) Trading Abroad
  • Trading of Refined Oil Products
  • Raw Materials and Industrial Products

Crude Plastics and Nylon Trading

  • Basic Steel Products Trading
  • Raw Materials for Industrial Use

Construction Equipment and Machinery
